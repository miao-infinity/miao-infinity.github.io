About

I'm a researcher in Robotics with a soft spot for Reinforcement Learning. After earning my M.Sc. in Dynamic Systems & Controls at Boston University, I spent three years as a Ph.D. student in Advanced Robot Manipulators Lab at Stevens Institute of Technology before pursuing my nothing but absolutely awesome startup journey.

I'm interested in developing algorithms that make robots learn faster and more efficiently for complex tasks in real-world environments, at the intersection of reinforcement learning, multi-agent systems, and control theory.

I love rock climbing (V6 bouldering / 5.12 sport) and was a competitive table tennis athlete on the Shanxi provincial team in China. The spirit of calligraphy still lingers in my heart.

My name "淼" means "boundlessness of the ocean" in Chinese, pronounced: "meow".
